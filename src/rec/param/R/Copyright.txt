Partially modified audio material from the Perceptual Voice Quality Database (PVQD) (https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/9dz247gnyb/4).
Walden, Patrick R. (2022), “Perceptual Voice Qualities Database (PVQD)”, Mendeley Data, V4, doi: 10.17632/9dz247gnyb.4

The material was used in the study "Voice Quality and Dypshonia" (https://osf.io/dxc2e/overview?view_only=7e25f2b0991a4322997dd4ce99858262), to which this software is connected.
